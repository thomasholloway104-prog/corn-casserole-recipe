Corn Casserole

A creamy, cheesy, and comforting corn casserole perfect for holidays or any cozy dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 can creamed corn
  • 1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Pour into greased baking dish.
  4. Bake for 45–50 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Let cool slightly and serve warm.
