Corn Casserole

A warm, cheesy, and creamy side dish made with sweet corn, cornbread mix, and just a handful of ingredients. A family favorite for holidays or any cozy meal!

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 oz) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (15 oz) can creamed corn
  • 1 (8.5 oz) box Jiffy corn muffin mix
  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) melted butter
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease an 8x8-inch baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the corn, creamed corn, Jiffy mix, melted butter, and sour cream until well combined.
  3. If using cheese, stir half into the mixture and save the rest for topping.
  4. Pour the mixture into the prepared baking dish and spread evenly.
  5. Top with remaining cheese if desired.
  6. Bake uncovered for 45–50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the center is set.
  7. Let cool slightly before serving. Enjoy!
